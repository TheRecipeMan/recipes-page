  • Biscuits and Gravy Recipe

This recipe is for when you want something that is relatively simple and easy to make..

Cooking Information
Prep Time Bake Time Cook Time Servings
6 mins 25 mins 15 mins 6

Ingredients

  • 6 frozen biscuits (you can make your own if you want).
  • 16 oz of breakfast sausage or chorizo
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of flour
  • salt
  • pepper
  • red pepper flakes

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375 degrees.
  2. When adequately heated, put your biscuits on a square pan or aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes (or according to instructions).
  3. While those is baking, get out a pot and turn it to medium high heat.
  4. Put your sausage in the pot and cook until brown.
  5. When it is browned, put your flour in and mix until the sausage is covered.
  6. Turn the heat to medium low and add your two cups milk slowly and while add it, stir.
  7. When you are finished, turn the heat to low and let it simmer.
  8. When your biscuits are done, plate them and your sausage gravy.
  9. Enjoy!
