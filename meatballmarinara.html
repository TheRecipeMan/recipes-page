Homemade Meatball Marinara

Even though meatball sandwiches from Subway are good, I think these will do if you want a homemade meatball marinara.

Cooking Information
Prep Time Bake Time Cook Time Servings
15 mins 20 mins 18 mins 9

Ingredients

  • 3lbs of ground beef
  • 16 oz of Italian sausage
  • 2 packs of Martin's Hoagie Rolls
  • 48 oz of Rao's Arrabiata (spicy marinara) sauce
  • 9 slices of provolone cheese.
  • 8 oz of parmesan cheese.
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tbsp garlic paste
  • 3 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Parsley as a garnish

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine your ground beef and sausage with the seasonings above.
  2. Roll them out into 27 small balls and place them on a rectangular baking pan, lined with aluminum foil.
  3. Bake on 375 degrees for 20 minutes.
  4. While those are baking, get a pot and put your sauce in, with the seasonings above along with the garlic paste.
  5. Put the sauce on medium heat.
  6. When your meatballs are finished baking, put them in the sauce and turn the heat to low or simmer.
  7. When you feel that they are done, turn the oven on a high broil.
  8. Get your hoagie roll, put 3 meatballs in it, top with extra sauce, provolone cheese, parmesan cheese.
  9. Put it in the oven for one or two minutes. Do not broil for longer!
